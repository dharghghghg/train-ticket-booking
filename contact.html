Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a train ticket?

Simply go to the "Search Trains" page, select your source and destination, choose your preferred train, fill in passenger details, and confirm your booking. You'll receive a PNR number for your booking.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes! Visit the "My Bookings" page, find your booking, and click the "Cancel Booking" button. Your booking will be cancelled immediately.

How many passengers can I book for in one ticket?

You can book tickets for up to 4 passengers in a single booking. Use the "Add Another Passenger" button on the booking page.
